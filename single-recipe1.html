Chocolate Cake

This chocolate cake is a celebration of rich flavors and moist texture that will delight every chocolate lover. It's perfect for birthdays, special occasions, or simply to satisfy your sweet cravings. Let’s embark on this delicious baking journey together!

Prep time

20 min.

Bake time

30 min.

Servings

8 servings

Tags : cake dessert chocolate

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.

Step 2

In a large mixing bowl, combine 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour, 1 ½ cups sugar, ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder, 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder, 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda, and 1 teaspoon salt. Mix well.

Step 3

Add 2 large eggs, 1 cup whole milk, ½ cup vegetable oil, and 2 teaspoons vanilla extract. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes until well combined.

Step 4

Stir in 1 cup of boiling water (the batter will be thin). Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.

Step 5

Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely on a wire rack.

Ingredients

1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour

1 ½ cups granulated sugar

¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder

1 ½ teaspoons baking powder

1 ½ teaspoons baking soda

1 teaspoon salt

2 large eggs

1 cup whole milk

½ cup vegetable oil

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

1 cup boiling water

Tools

Mixing Bowls

Electric Mixer

Measuring Cups

Measuring Spoons

Two 9-inch Round Cake Pans

Cooling Rack
