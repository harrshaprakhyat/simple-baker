Blueberry Muffin

Delight in these fluffy blueberry muffins that are bursting with juicy blueberries in every bite! Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as a snack, these muffins are sure to become a favorite in your home. Let's bake together!

Prep time

15 min.

Bake time

20 min.

Servings

12 muffins

Tags : muffin breakfast blueberry

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease with cooking spray.

Step 2

In a large bowl, whisk together 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 tablespoon baking powder, and ½ teaspoon salt.

Step 3

In another bowl, mix ½ cup granulated sugar, 1 large egg, ½ cup whole milk, and ½ cup melted butter until combined.

Step 4

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Gently fold in 1 cup fresh blueberries.

Step 5

Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about ¾ full.

Step 6

Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let them cool for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon baking powder

½ teaspoon salt

½ cup granulated sugar

1 large egg

½ cup whole milk

½ cup melted butter

1 cup fresh blueberries

Tools

Mixing Bowls

Muffin Tin

Whisk

Measuring Cups

Measuring Spoons
